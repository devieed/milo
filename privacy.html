Milo (package name: net.zrex.milo) respects your privacy. This policy explains how Z-Rex Network (“we,” “us”) collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects personal information when you use the Milo mobile app and related services, and what choices you have.

By using the Service, you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, do not use the Service. This policy works together with our Terms of Service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide

  • Account data: Email address; if you use Google sign-in, your Google account identifier (Google ID), email, and display name from Google.
  • Profile data: Nickname, avatar (chosen from in-app presets), bio, primary language, zodiac sign, birth decade range, and gender (you may keep gender private).
  • User content: Letters, replies, conversation messages, and associated virtual identity nicknames and avatars.
  • Social actions: Pen pal requests, accept/decline records, pen pal relationships, and visibility settings.

1.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device and log data: To keep the Service secure and stable, our servers may log access times, API requests, and error logs. When sending email verification codes, we may log and rate-limit your IP address to prevent abuse.
  • App activity: Check-in history, virtual stamp balance and changes, letter send/receive status, conversation and read status, and rewarded ad claim records—for core features and anti-abuse.
  • Authentication tokens: After sign-in, your device may store access tokens (JWT) and refresh tokens locally to keep you signed in.

1.3 Device permissions (Android)

The Milo Android app (net.zrex.milo) may request device permissions declared in the app package or invoked at runtime by the operating system, our app framework (uni-app), or integrated SDKs (such as Google Sign-In and advertising). We request only what is needed for the purposes below. You can deny or revoke many permissions in Android Settings; some features may not work if you do.

Permission / feature Why it may be used
INTERNET (network access) Sign in, send and receive letters, sync mailbox data, load rewarded ads, and call our APIs.
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE, ACCESS_WIFI_STATE, CHANGE_NETWORK_STATE, CHANGE_WIFI_STATE Detect whether you are online, choose appropriate network behavior, and recover after connectivity changes.
WAKE_LOCK Keep the app responsive during sign-in, email verification, and rewarded video playback so sessions are not interrupted.
VIBRATE Optional haptic feedback for actions such as check-in and sending a letter.
CAMERA, FLASHLIGHT, camera hardware features May be declared by the app framework or third-party SDKs. Milo is primarily text-based today. Camera access may be used if you choose future or optional features (for example taking a profile photo). We do not access the camera in the background.
Photos, images, and videos (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES, READ_MEDIA_VIDEO, or legacy storage access on older Android versions) Requested only when you use a feature that needs media—for example selecting a photo from your gallery for a profile picture, attaching an image to a letter (if enabled), or saving a share image to your device. We do not scan your entire photo library; access is limited to items you pick.
GET_ACCOUNTS On some Android versions, used by Google Sign-In to show the account picker when you choose “Continue with Google.”
READ_PHONE_STATE May be included by the app framework or advertising SDK for fraud prevention, security, and delivery measurement. We do not use this to identify you for marketing and do not sell this data.
READ_LOGS, MOUNT_UNMOUNT_FILESYSTEMS, WRITE_SETTINGS Legacy or framework-level declarations from the uni-app runtime. Milo does not change your system settings or mount storage for its core letter features.

What we do not do by default: Milo does not require precise GPS location, contacts, microphone, or SMS access for core text letter features. If we add new permissions, we will update this policy and explain them before or when they are requested.

1.4 What we do not require for core use

You can use Milo’s main text letter and pen pal features without granting camera, photo library, or phone-state access unless a specific feature or SDK on your device requests it at runtime.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  1. Create and manage your account and verify identity (including email codes);
  2. Deliver, store, and display letters and conversations, including drifting, delayed delivery, and pen pal features;
  3. Run content safety reviews and handle violations;
  4. Issue and manage virtual stamps, check-in rewards, and ad rewards;
  5. Protect the Service from spam, abuse, and attacks;
  6. Improve the product through aggregated or de-identified analytics;
  7. Respond to your requests and support inquiries;
  8. Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

3. Content Moderation and Automated Decisions

When you send a letter or reply, we run local sensitive-word checks and may send the text to a third-party moderation service (currently the OpenAI Moderation API) for automated analysis. Results may affect whether content can be sent.

If you disagree with a moderation outcome, contact support@z-rex.net for a manual review. We will respond within reason but cannot guarantee reinstatement of rejected content.

4. Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only as follows:

4.1 Service providers

  • Google: Google account sign-in (OAuth) and Google AdMob rewarded ads. Google may process device advertising identifiers per its policies.
    See: Google Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI: Text content moderation. We send text pending review; we do not intentionally send your email or account ID to OpenAI (but text may include information you write yourself).
    See: OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Email delivery: To send login verification codes—limited to your email and data needed to deliver mail.
  • Cloud infrastructure: Databases, caches (e.g., MariaDB, Redis), and hosting providers that store or process data on our behalf.

4.2 Other users

  • Before you become pen pals, others typically see only your virtual identity, not your registration email.
  • After becoming pen pals, others may see profile fields you make visible.
  • Recipients see content you choose to send in letters and replies.

4.3 Legal and safety

We may disclose information when required by law, valid legal process, or when necessary to protect users, the public, or our rights.

5. Storage and International Transfers

  • Data is primarily stored on servers in regions we use for operations. Locations may change as our infrastructure evolves.
  • Because we use providers in different countries (e.g., Google, OpenAI), some data may be processed outside your country, including outside mainland China. We take reasonable steps to protect transfers and limit them to what is needed for the purposes in this policy.
  • We retain information only as long as needed for those purposes. After account deletion, we delete or anonymize personal data within a reasonable period, except where law requires retention.

6. Security

We use measures such as HTTPS, access controls, and hashed password storage where applicable. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. If an incident may affect your personal information, we will notify you and take remedial steps as required by law.

7. Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • Access and correct profile data in the app (Profile → Edit);
  • Delete content where the product supports it;
  • Delete your account via our account deletion page or by emailing support@z-rex.net from your registered address. After email confirmation, your account is blocked immediately; personal data is permanently deleted and anonymized after a 7-day grace period;
  • Withdraw consent by stopping use or deleting your account—without affecting processing that already occurred;
  • Complain to us or to a competent data protection authority if you believe your rights were violated.

8. Children

The Service is intended for users aged 16 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we collected a child’s data without consent, contact support@z-rex.net and we will delete it promptly.

For our published standards against child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), see our Child Safety Standards.

9. Cookies and Local Storage

The Milo app mainly uses on-device storage (tokens, language preference) rather than browser cookies. This legal site is static HTML; we do not set tracking cookies here. Hosting platforms (GitHub Pages, Cloudflare Pages, etc.) may keep basic access logs—see their documentation.
